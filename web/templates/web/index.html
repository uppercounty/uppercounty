Upper County Dolphins

We are a community swim team with the Montgomery County Recreation program. We compete in Division A of the Montgomery County Swim League (MCSL) on Saturday mornings and in a local B league on Wednesday nights. We welcome swimmers up to ages 18 who live in the nearby neighborhoods and communities near the Upper County pool. If you're looking for fun and fast times in and out of the pool, you've found the best team around!
